Insert

Insert works the same for both rows and columns. Select a cell next to the point of where you wish to insert. Then select either Insert Row or Insert Column from the menu or toolbar. The column will be inserted to the left of the selected cell, the row will be inserted above the point. Selecting two cells in different columns will result in two columns being inserted, and likewise for rows.
